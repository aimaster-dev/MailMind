Graduation gowns can be collected from June 10 to June 15, between 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM, at Hall A, Building 3.

To collect a gown for someone else, bring a signed authorization letter and both student IDs.

Gown sizes available include XS, S, M, L, and XL.

All gowns must be returned by June 30 to avoid a $50 late fee.

Damaged or lost gowns incur a $200 replacement fee.

Questions can be emailed to gown-support@university.edu.
